The Hospital Assets Management System (HAMS) is developed by the Regional Director of Health Services (RDHS) Office to effectively manage, track, and monitor inventories including medical equipment, furniture, and healthcare assets.

This centralized system covers all healthcare institutions under the RDHS Office — including Base Hospitals, Divisional Hospitals, Primary Medical Care Units (PMCUs), and other field institutions.

HAMS is designed to enhance accountability, improve asset tracking, and simplify reporting and decision-making processes across the region.
