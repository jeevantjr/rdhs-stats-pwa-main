About Us

Regional Directorate of Health Services – Demo Region

Our Vision

To be the excellent organization in Demo Region to create a Healthier Community that contributes towards a Healthier Nation.

Our Mission

To assure a healthier community by ensuring equitable access to sustainable quality healthcare services while promoting self-reliance, personal responsibility, and healthy lifestyles in Demo Region.

Our Core Services

Curative Care Services

  • Planning, implementing, and monitoring of curative healthcare services.
  • Maintaining medical statistics and regulating private healthcare.
  • Supporting events like hospital upgrades and emergency units.
  • Coordinating technical planning and research efforts with the Ministry of Health.

Preventive Health Services

  • Strategic planning and implementation of preventive programs.
  • Supporting MOH units across the district.
  • Delivering services through PHM, SPHM, PHNS, PHI, SPHI.
  • Health surveys, monitoring, and program evaluation.

Other Services

  • Maternal and Child Health – including antenatal care and nutrition.
  • School Health – provided through MOH and school dental therapists.
  • Well-Women Services – for free medical screening of women aged 35+.
  • Family Planning – providing modern contraceptive methods free of charge.
  • Occupational Health, Communicable & NCD Control, Mental Health, Oral Care.
